Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Dayflux is designed with privacy at its core.

Last Updated: January 2025

ðŸ”’ Privacy First Design

Dayflux stores all your data locally on your device. We don't have servers, we don't track you, and we can't see your breathing sessions or wellness data. Your journey is yours alone.

1. Information Collection

Dayflux is designed to respect your privacy:

  • No Account Required: You can use Dayflux without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Local Storage: All breathing session data, progress tracking, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Core Data.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
  • No Ads: Dayflux is ad-free and always will be.

2. Data Storage

Local Data

Your breathwork and wellness data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Breathing session records and durations
  • Progress tracking and consistency data
  • Preferred breathing techniques and patterns
  • App preferences and settings
  • Session history and statistics

Optional Cloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization:

  • Your data is encrypted by Apple's iCloud service
  • We cannot access your synced data
  • Data is synced only between your own devices
  • You can disable this feature at any time

3. Data Sharing

We have a simple policy: We don't share your data because we don't have it.

  • No breathing session data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is sold or used for marketing
  • No data leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
  • No health data is collected or transmitted

4. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Export your progress data at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual sessions or all data instantly
  • Reset: Start fresh whenever you want
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all local data
  • Share Selectively: Choose what session data to share with healthcare providers if desired

5. Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS's built-in security features
  • Device passcode/biometric protection
  • Encrypted device storage
  • Optional iCloud end-to-end encryption
  • No network transmission of sensitive data

6. Health Data

Dayflux is designed to be health-conscious:

  • We do not access or store any HealthKit data
  • No biometric data is collected
  • Session data remains within the app
  • You control any integration with health apps

7. Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • They are scheduled locally on your device
  • No data is sent to push notification servers
  • You can customize or disable them anytime
  • No personal information is included in notifications

8. Audio and Visual Content

Regarding our guided content:

  • Guided audio sessions are stored locally on your device
  • No audio is recorded from your device
  • Visual breathing guides are generated locally
  • No session recordings are created or stored

9. Children's Privacy

Dayflux does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app stores all data locally and requires no personal information to function. Children should use breathing exercises under adult supervision.

10. International Users

For users outside of Australia:

  • The same privacy protections apply globally
  • Local data storage means compliance with most privacy laws
  • No cross-border data transfers occur
  • Regional privacy rights are respected

11. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you through the app if changes are significant
  • Always maintain our commitment to privacy-first design
  • Never compromise on local data storage principles

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy or how Dayflux works:

  • Email: dev.blsmdrive@gmail.com
  • Visit our Support page

Our Promise

Dayflux will always be private by design. We believe your breathwork practice and wellness journey are deeply personal, and your data should remain that way. Your breath, your data, your control.
